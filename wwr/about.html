History

Whitewater rafting, as a recreational sport, gained significant popularity in the 1960s and 70s, with commercial companies playing a key role in its development and spread. These companies, driven by a passion for the outdoors, helped to popularize rafting and introduce it to a wider audience, particularly through trips in iconic locations like the Grand Canyon. The sport's visibility was further boosted by its inclusion in the 1972 Munich Olympics and subsequent appearances in later games.
